SEO Re-Analysis: Bizmri.ai Before vs. After

Competitive positioning update after deploying 25 blog pages · March 9, 2026

Executive Summary

With the addition of 25 SEO-optimized blog pages, Bizmri.ai has gone from near-zero content presence to a meaningful content footprint covering 25 high-value keywords across pillar guides, competitor comparisons, thought leadership, and feature pages. The site's indexable page count has jumped from ~5 to ~31, total content from ~740 words to ~31,500 words, and keyword coverage from 5 of 25 target keywords to 25 of 25.

However, the homepage itself remains unchanged — the same technical SEO gaps identified in the first audit (missing canonical tag, no structured data, brand-only H1) are still present. The blog pages address the content and keyword gap, but the homepage still needs the quick-win fixes.

Overall Status: Significant Improvement — from "Needs Significant Work" to "Foundation Built, Ready to Scale."

What's Still Missing (Remaining Gaps)

Still Needs Fixing (Homepage)

  • No canonical tag on homepage
